Privacy & responsible disclosure

What we collect

AbuseRadar publicly indexes, classifies and stores backlinks, DNS records, certificate transparency entries and rendered HTML/screenshots of sites suspected of hosting injected gambling, pharma or malware content. We do not collect personal browsing data from visitors of compromised sites.

If your site is listed

We almost certainly listed it because automated detection flagged compromised content on a domain you operate. Email disclose@abuseradar.org with the URL and we will: share the exact evidence package, walk you through removal, and re-verify within 24 hours. We will not publicly disclose individual victim domains without coordinated remediation.

If you operate one of the attacker domains

You will not hear from us. You will hear from your registrar, your hosting provider, the relevant CERT, Google Safe Browsing and — depending on victim sector — national law enforcement liaison.

Site analytics

This website (abuseradar.org) does not use trackers, third-party analytics or advertising cookies. The only outbound requests are to Google Fonts for typography. Email subscribers are stored solely for the briefing they requested and never shared.
