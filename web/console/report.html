6. Remediation playbook

  1. Locate the injection — search the site's footer/header/sidebar templates for the absolutely-positioned hidden link cluster (typical CSS: position:absolute;left:-9999px or opacity:0;height:0). DOM excerpt is in the bundle.
  2. Audit recently modified files — look for backdoor PHP web-shells under /wp-content/, /admin/ or template directories. Disable any out-of-date CMS plugins.
  3. Rotate credentials — change all CMS administrator passwords, FTP/SSH keys. Re-deploy from a clean backup if possible.
  4. Notify AbuseRadar — reply to the breach notification email and we re-render the page within 12 hours to confirm closure.
