7. Remediation playbook

  1. Locate the injection — search the site's footer / header / sidebar templates for the absolutely-positioned hidden link cluster (typical CSS: position:absolute;left:-9999px, opacity:0;height:0, or display:none). The DOM excerpt in Section 6 shows the exact lines.
  2. Audit recently modified files — look for backdoor PHP web-shells under /wp-content/, /admin/, or template directories. Disable any out-of-date CMS plugins.
  3. Rotate credentials — change all CMS administrator passwords, FTP/SSH keys. If possible, redeploy from a known-clean backup.
  4. Verify closure — reply to team@abuseradar.org (or the original notification email). AbuseRadar re-renders the page within 12 hours and confirms.
